Keyword Research & SEO Strategy

I specialize in identifying high-value keywords that balance search volume, difficulty, and relevance to the client’s goals. By analyzing data from tools like Ubersuggest, I build structured keyword lists and categorize them into primary, supporting, and local segments to drive both organic visibility and targeted traffic.

Data-Driven Insights – I extract, clean, and organize keyword data into actionable lists with search volume, difficulty, and location.

Strategic Categorization – I classify keywords into core business terms, supporting content opportunities, and local/geo-focused phrases, ensuring a full-funnel SEO approach.

Trend & Competitor Analysis – I track search trends over time and analyze competitors’ rankings to uncover gaps and opportunities.

Result-Oriented Planning – My research directly informs content strategy, link-building, and campaign planning, helping brands rank for terms that bring real traffic and conversions.

Data Researching for Content

Validated, data-driven facts form the foundation for stronger content creation, helping businesses establish credibility, improve SEO visibility, and provide content teams with ready-to-use insights.
